ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Rooted in faith, reaching the world. Learn how Kāula Tree grew from humble beginnings in Hawai'i to a global prophetic ministry.

HOW IT STARTED

Born in Hawai'i,
Called to the World

Kāula Tree was established in 2023 by Ben Eberhart and Stanton Sunaoka in Hawai'i. What started as prayer meetings in their home grew into something neither of them could have imagined — a global ministry dedicated to prophetic outreach and training.

Drawing from experiences abroad and a deep commitment to following where God leads, they saw a need for a ministry that would awaken people to the reality that God still speaks today — personally, powerfully, and with purpose.

The name "Kāula" comes from the Hawaiian word for prophet. Like the deep-rooted trees of the islands, Kāula Tree is grounded in biblical truth while reaching out to communities around the world.

OUR MISSION

To Awaken the World
to a God Who Speaks.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our Core Values

ENCOURAGE

Prophecy is for strengthening, encouraging, and comforting (1 Cor. 14:3). We speak life into people's situations, reminding them of God's love and the plans He has for their lives.

EQUIP

We train and activate believers in the prophetic gifts, providing practical tools and hands-on experience to establish a prophetic culture wherever God leads.

ALOHA

As Paul reminds us in 1 Corinthians 13, without love it's all worth nothing. We lead with aloha in everything we do because the gifts only matter when they flow from a heart of love.
