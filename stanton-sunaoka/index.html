Stanton Sunaoka

Stanton Sunaoka is the co-founder of Kāula Tree, a prophetic ministry nonprofit based in Hawai'i. Born and raised in the islands, Stanton carries a deep passion for seeing people encounter God in a personal and powerful way.

From an early age, Stanton felt a calling to ministry. His journey has taken him across the globe, where he has served in various capacities — from leading worship to prophetic ministry, and from community outreach to one-on-one discipleship.

"God is speaking — all the time. Our job is to help people tune in and hear what He's saying over their lives."

In 2023, alongside Ben Eberhart, Stanton co-founded Kāula Tree with a vision to awaken the world to a God who speaks. His leadership is marked by authenticity, a heart for the nations, and a deep commitment to biblical truth.

Stanton's ministry is rooted in the belief that the prophetic gifts are for today and for every believer. He is passionate about equipping others to step into their calling and walk boldly in the gifts God has given them.

When he's not traveling for ministry, you'll find Stanton in Kaneohe, Hawai'i — surfing, spending time with friends and family, and serving his local community.
