ABOUT US

Rooted in Faith,
Reaching the World

Kāula Tree was established in 2023 by Ben Eberhart and Stanton Sunaoka in Hawai'i. What started as prayer meetings in their home grew into a global ministry dedicated to prophetic outreach and training.

Drawing from experiences abroad and a deep commitment to following where God leads, Kāula Tree has become a platform for strengthening believers and equipping them in the prophetic worldwide.
