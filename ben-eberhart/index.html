Ben Eberhart

Ben Eberhart is the co-founder of Kāula Tree, a prophetic ministry nonprofit established in 2023 in Hawai'i. With a background in ministry and a deep love for the nations, Ben has dedicated his life to helping others encounter God in transformative ways.

Ben's journey into prophetic ministry began through years of international missions work and serving in various church settings. These experiences shaped his understanding of how God speaks and ignited a passion to equip others in the prophetic gifts.

"Everyone has a story that God wants to speak into. Our calling is to help people hear what He's already been saying."

Together with Stanton Sunaoka, Ben founded Kāula Tree with the vision of awakening the world to a God who speaks. His approach to ministry is grounded in Scripture, fueled by compassion, and marked by a deep desire to see people walk in their God-given purpose.

Ben brings a unique combination of strategic thinking and pastoral heart to the ministry. He believes that the prophetic is not just for a few — it's for every believer who is willing to listen. Through Kāula Tree's trips, teachings, and training programs, he works to make that vision a reality.

Ben is based in Kaneohe, Hawai'i, where he and the Kāula Tree team continue to grow the ministry's local and global impact.
